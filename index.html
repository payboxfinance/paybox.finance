About us

Paybox is led by a group of developers and technology professionals who are passionate about the potential of decentralized applications. The Paybox Application and smart contracts are designed to fill the void for token payments and peer to peer exchange in a way that can revolutionize how we settle

In 2021 crypto currencies came into more light than ever, and its quickly not just becoming something that is used for investments only, many people are already today receiving their salaries in crypto, the defi space alone have seen an incredible growth of money pouring into it recently. Clearly there is an increasing demand for payments in crypto, without the need of always bouncing between fiat and crypto, in many countries people are already used to a more or less cashless way of living, they are daily using all sorts of apps to make payments, the step to crypto is not far. Projects like Paybox are built on the foundation of decentralization and we are developing Paybox to help tear down long-established ideals and reinventing new more efficient ways of doing things.

Paybox utilises smart contracts that gives a more secure environment, and piece of mind to do payment and exchanges over the blockchain. The Paybox cloud service itself will make it easier for everyone to take advantage of using crypto over other traditional payment solutions. Most who already discovered the benefits of crypto are now eagerly awaiting when these solutions will be available.

The strengths our team brings to Paybox include over 10 years experience developing software applications integrated with many of the existing payment solutions available throughout the internet, and were very excited to build our own solution based on todays blockchains that people use.

MISSION : its to deliver a secure payment system that allows for anyone to accept payments and exchange tokens effortlessly.
